Subah uthte hi kamar me jakkad kyun hoti hai?

### Common Reasons
- Galat sleeping posture  
- Kam movement  
- Weak back muscles  

### Simple Exercise
Cat–Camel exercise roz subah karein.

### Kab Physiotherapist ko dikhaye?
Agar 2–3 hafton me pain kam na ho.
